Amish Beef and Noodles

Description

This sweet potato cheesecake has a smooth, creamy texture with a buttery crumb crust and delectable pecan praline topping.

Perfect for a rich and flavorful fall or winter dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1¼ cup white sugar

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C ).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and 1/4 cup melted butter together in a bowl until combined. Press crumb mixture firmly and evenly across the bottom and slightly up the sides of a 9 ½-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes; remove from oven and set aside. Keep the oven on.
